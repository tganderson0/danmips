Commands

  • add destReg, reg1, reg2
  • sub destReg, reg1, reg2
  • and destReg, reg1, reg2
  • or destReg, reg1, reg2
  • slt destReg, reg1, reg2
  • lw destReg, addressRegister, offset
  • sw addressRegister, valueRegister, offset
  • beq reg1, reg2, destLabel
  • j destLabel
  • addi destReg, reg1, immediate
  • print register (prints value of register to console, for debugging, not used in binary output)

Please note, for loading and storing, the program will be angry if you don't do offsets in multiples of 4

Additionally, while this will convert the DanMIPS code into binary, I do not have it working to convert into hex. The conversion at this point should be fairly easy though, just split the thing in half and make it hex
